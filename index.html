Common questions

Everything you want to know before you call

How much does a custom outdoor kitchen cost?

Most outdoor kitchens we build in St. Johns County range from $15,000 to $60,000+ depending on size, layout, materials, and appliances. We provide a detailed itemized quote after your free consultation — no guessing, no surprise charges. We're also an Elite Preferred Builder for Lyon Financial if financing makes sense for your situation.

How long does the build take?

Most projects complete within 6 to 10 weeks from groundbreak. We give you a realistic, written timeline upfront and keep you updated throughout the build. We don't disappear after you sign.

Do I need permits for an outdoor kitchen in Florida?

In most cases, yes — and that's in your best interest. A permitted build protects your resale value and ensures everything is done to code. As a State Certified Building Contractor (CBC-1263763), we handle all permitting so you don't have to.

What materials hold up best in Florida's climate?

Florida's heat, humidity, and rain destroy the wrong materials fast. We only use weather-resistant stone, concrete, and marine-grade components built for this climate. Come to our showroom and we'll show you exactly what we recommend — and why — side by side.

What appliance brands do you install?

We're an authorized dealer for Delta Heat and Twin Eagles — two of the most respected outdoor cooking appliance brands available. You can see both in our showroom before committing. If you have a preferred brand, let us know and we'll accommodate it.

Is the consultation really free? What's the catch?

There is no catch. We've built our entire reputation in St. Augustine on honest, no-pressure conversations. The consultation costs you nothing. We're a BBB Accredited business — our reputation in this community is everything to us, and we earn it one happy customer at a time.
